FAQ

1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
  1. Go to HZ WebPrint
  2. choose the file you want to print
  3. click on next
  4. choose the HZ printer
  5. go to the printer and use your HZ student card to login then choose print.
2. How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?
  1. Login with your HZ student card
  2. Choose scan.
3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event at Friday 16/09) on the HZ web shop?
  1. Go to the hz webshop
  2. choose the item you want
  3. make your payment (you can pay via ideal).
4. Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?
  1. Go to HZ Actualiteiten
